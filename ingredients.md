# ChickenNoodleSoup
1 tablespoon vegetable oil

1 onion, chopped

2 stalks celery, sliced

2 carrots, sliced

4 (10.5 ounce) cans chicken broth

4 baking potatoes, peeled and diced

¼ pinch dried thyme

¼ teaspoon poultry seasoning

2 cups shredded cooked chicken

2 cups egg noodles

1 cup evaporated milk
